SNORKELLING

There are many places that you can go snorkelling and diving in Malaysia. Places:

  • Perhentian Islands
  • Sipadan Islands

SIGHTSEEING

There are many tourist attractions in Malaysia that are amazing for sightseeing! Most notable attractions are:

  • Petronas Twin Towers (Kuala Lumpur)
  • Gunung Mulu National Park
  • A'Famosa Fortress (Melaka)
  • Dutch Square (Melaka)

TREKKING

You can also enjoy both hiking and trekking in Malaysia. You can visit Mount Kinabalu National Park to hike on the mountain trails or Gunung Mulu National Park as well. Both locations have picturesque views as you trek along the routes.
